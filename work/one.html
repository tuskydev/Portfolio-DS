Python Game of Life

This project uses metamorphosis as the lifeline of the cells serving as the pivotal force that governs their evolution and sustenance.

Github  |  Go Back

Goal

Simulating cell evolution using simple rules, exploring emergent behaviors and patterns. Through iterative updates based on neighboring cells, it aims to showcase complex dynamics from basic principles.

Result

Self-organization in cellular automata. Through visualization and analysis, the outcomes illustrate the fascinating dynamics of living systems arising from fundamentals.

Project Duration

Project duration varies between 2 weeks and 3 months. In order to carry out the project as quickly as possible, it is important that the relevant knowledge is correct and efficient.

Case - How can I optimize evolution of the cellular automaton?

Often, experiment with configurations and rules to enhance stability and efficiency. Through analysis, we aim to uncover patterns guiding better strategies for managing complex systems. rough this process, we seek to develop strategies that promote stability, efficiency, and emergent properties, thereby enhancing the overall performance and adaptability of the cellular automaton.
